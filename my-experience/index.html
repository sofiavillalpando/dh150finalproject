My Experience

This past October I created a website that tells the story of my journey to accepting and empowering my identity. I included background about myself, my journey at UCLA, an About Me page, and resources for any readers who seek them. I created my website intending to share my story to help other mixed students see that they are not alone and that there are resources for them at UCLA and on the internet. I want to use this website  and the one below to spread awareness about how mixed individuals are often conflicted with their identities, and share my story on how I embraced mine in hopes of inspiring others to do the same. For more information about me and my journey, check it out below:

About Me

Hi! My name is Sofia Villalpando and I am a third-year undergraduate Statistics major and Digital Humanities minor student at UCLA. Being mixed has always been a struggle for me, I never felt like I belonged in either culture of my background. I created this site with the intention of encouraging mixed students to find their identity and to reassure them that they are not alone. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn!
